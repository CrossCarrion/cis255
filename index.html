  4. I think that 000webhost is the best for keeping information about articles. The UI is more user friendly when compared to the other two. CSIS doesn't really have a UI and requires jumping through hoops with either Notepad++ or an FTP application. M-Drive's UI is just a little clunky. I had trouble figuring out how to close a pop-up window when uploading files. Also, 000webhost is a bigger company and is likely to have more uptime than either CSIS or M-Drive both of which are hosted via SVSU who is notorious for network problems.
